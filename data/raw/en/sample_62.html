Daily Dunya (Urdu: روزنامہ دُنیا) is an Urdu daily newspaper from Pakistan. It was launched on 3 September 2012 by National Communication Services from Lahore. It is published simultaneously from Karachi, Lahore, Faisalabad, Gujranwala, Multan, Quetta and Sargodha.[2] Daily Dunya launched Sheikh Rasheed Ahmad's second book "Lal Haveli Sey Akwaam-e-Mutahidda Tak" (From Lal Haveli to UNO).[3]

It is owned by Mian Amer Mahmood who is also the owner of Dunya News and Lahore News HD.
