Genus of fungi

Wawea
Scientific classification
Domain: Eukaryota
Kingdom: Fungi
Division: Ascomycota
Class: Lecanoromycetes
Order: Baeomycetales
Family: Arctomiaceae
Genus: Wawea

Type species
Wawea fruticulosa
Henssen & Kantvilas

Wawea is a genus of lichenized fungi within the Arctomiaceae family.[1] This is a monotypic genus, containing the single species Wawea fruticulosa.[2]
