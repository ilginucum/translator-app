united states sharpshooters was a sharpshooter regiment that served in the union army during the american civil war from to january they were members of the first iron brigade also known as the iron brigade of the east serviceedit the nd us sharpshooters was raised as follows company a in minnesota on october company b in michigan on october company c in pennsylvania on october company d in maine on november company e in vermont on november company f in new hampshire on november company g in new hampshire on december company h in vermont on december the nd and the st united states volunteer sharpshooter regiment were consolidated on december and the regiment was broken up on february and the remaining companies distributed as follows company a transferred to the st minnesota volunteer infantry regiment company b transferred to the th michigan volunteer infantry regiment company c transferred to the th pennsylvania volunteer infantry regiment company d transferred to the th maine volunteer infantry regiment company f transferred to the th new hampshire volunteer infantry company g transferred to the th new hampshire volunteer infantry company h transferred to the th vermont
