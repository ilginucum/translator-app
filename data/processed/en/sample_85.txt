road bicycle racing representing the united states olympic games los angeles team time trial ronald alexander kiefel born april in denver is a former professional road bicycle racer from the united states kiefel is a seventime tour de france racer olympic bronze medalist and member of the united states bicycling hall of fame kiefel rode for american professional teams such as eleven motorola coors light and saturn his wins included the trofeo laigueglia and the tour of tuscany he became the first american stage winner in a grand tour when he won stage from laquila to perugia in the giro ditalia he competed in seven tours de france and represented the usa at the olympic games where he won bronze in the team time trial with roy knickman davis phinney and andy weaver in kiefel won the uspro road championship the time trial and the team time trial he was also road champion in he retired from racing in and has since commentated on tv and radio for european classics and tours he is a coach in wheat ridge colorado vice president of wheat ridge cyclery and promotes races and rides in kiefel was inducted in the united states bicycling hall of fame he and his wife meegan at one time
