bradshaw popham grandfather peter popham born january is an english journalist author and playwright he is best known for his journalism at the independent from to and his books on aung san suu kyi early lifeedit popham was born in cork ireland in his father was the poet novelist and military historian hugh popham raised in richmond surrey he was educated at latymer upper school where he edited the school magazine and was a contributor to the notorious schoolkids issue of the underground magazine oz education and early careeredit at the university of leeds where he read english he edited poetry and audience the universitys longestablished poetry magazine and launched leeds larynx a cultural review his first fiction to be published was collision and goodbye a short story in transatlantic review no in may after working in theatres in leeds london and bristol and writing more plays in he moved to japan where after teaching english for two years he became a freelance journalist and wrote his first book tokyo the city at the end of the world published by kodansha international in it was translated into japanese and published the same year by asahi shinbunsha as portrait of tokyo tky no shz at the independentedit in he joined the staff of the british newspaper the independent as feature writer on the independent magazine under alexander chancellor in he was appointed south asia correspondent of the newspaper and moved to delhi while living in delhi he drafted his novel across the great divide set before and after indias independence and partition in he moved to italy to become the rome correspondent in his study of aung san suu kyi the lady and the peacock was published by random house it was translated into dutch japanese korean chinese and mongolian and published in the us by the experiment a pirated edition appeared in burma on sale at the headquarters of suu kyis party the national league for democracy in yangon his sequel the lady and the generals was published by penguin random house in since leaving the independent in he has written several plays including the kingdom of heaven performed at the script room in london in february and savages in a zoom production directed by sebastian michael and starring tim bentinck in july selected worksedit popham peter tokyo the city at the end of the world kodansha international
